About me

I'm a software engineer and graduated from National Taiwan university. When I was a graduate student, my research was in the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Android OS, and Robotics. After I graduated, I started my professional career at HTC company to develop Android multimedia framework and applications. Then, in 2014, I joined Nvidia and worked on Android frameworks. In 2015, I was invited by ASUS to build the companion robot for home, and my duties include computer vision, deep learning, image geometry, devops, and software architecture on different platforms. In 2018, I joined Amazon Internationalization team to build services, tools, and libraries for i18n. After working for Amazon Internationalization for two years, I changed to Amazon Transportation services team to work on Yard management systems.
